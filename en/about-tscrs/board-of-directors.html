Board of Directors

Prof. Ayhan Kuzu M.D.
President

Ayhan Kuzu was born in Ankara in 1963. He completed his primary, secondary and high school education at the Turkish Education Association Ankara College. He graduated from Ankara University Faculty of Medicine in 1986.

Between 1991-1992, he worked as a general surgery specialist and research assistant in the Department of General Surgery at Leeds University School of Medicine in England. He received the title of General Surgery Specialist in 1993.

In 1998, he was appointed as a specialist to Ankara University Faculty of Medicine General Surgery Clinic. He was awarded the title of Associate Professor in November 1999 and Professor in May 2005.

Ayhan Kuzu, who has more than 150 articles in total, 77 of which have been published in international journals, has undertaken the task of representing our country many times as a guest speaker and panelist in international congresses as well as writing scientific papers.

Prof. Emre Balık M.D.
Vice President and Treasurer

Emre Balık was born in 1973 in Gaziantep. He graduated from Gaziantep Anatolian High School in 1991 and from Istanbul University Faculty of Medicine in 1997. He completed his general surgery specialty education again at Istanbul University Faculty of Medicine between 1997 and 2002. 

Between 2004 and 2005, he worked in the field of minimally invasive surgery at Columbia University, School of Medicine, in the colorectal surgery department. Between 2002 and 2010, he worked as a research assistant in the Department of General Surgery at Istanbul Faculty of Medicine and in 2010 he earned the title of associate professor.

In 2013, he passed the European Coloproctology proficiency exam. Working as a faculty member at Istanbul Faculty of Medicine until August 2014, Emre Balık has been working as a lecturer at Koç University School of Medicine, Department of General Surgery as of August 2014.

Prof. Bilgi Baca M.D.
Secretary

Bilgi Baca who graduated from Istanbul University Faculty of Medicine in 1996, completed his Specialization Training in Istanbul University Cerrahpaşa Faculty of Medicine, Department of General Surgery in 2003 with his thesis titled 'Can mechanical bowel cleaning before infrarenal aortic surgery reduce colonic ischemia / reperfusion damage?' Bilgi Baca received his Associate Professorship in 2009 from Istanbul University Cerrahpaşa Faculty of Medicine, Department of General Surgery.

In 2009 and 2010, he increased his experience by working in the Department of Colorectal Surgery (Colon and Rectal Surgery) at the Keck School of Medicine, Southern California University in Los Angeles, which has a reputable place in its field. Simultaneously, he attended courses and congresses organized by experts of General Surgery operations. After his experience abroad, he continued his profession and academic career in his country.

Bilgi Baca who got his Professorship in Acıbadem University Faculty of Medicine, Department of General Surgery, is currently working as a faculty member at Acıbadem University Faculty of Medicine, Department of General Surgery and as the Head of the Department of General Surgery at Acıbadem University Altunizade Hospital.

Prof. İlker Sücüllü M.D.
Education Committe Chair / Web Supervisor

İlker Sücüllü was born in 1972 in Denizli. From the GATA Military Medical Faculty in 1996, he graduated with the rank of G. Ml. Lieutenant. He completed his General Surgery Specialization training at GATA Haydarpaşa Training Hospital General Surgery Service between 1999-2004. He worked as a General Surgery Specialist in Ankara Gendarmerie Hospital and as a Chief Physician in Şırnak Military Hospital.

In 2006, he took up the position of Assistant Professor at GATA Haydarpaşa Training Hospital General Surgery Service. In 2010, he received training in Minimally Invasive Colorectal Surgery and Pelvic Floor Diseases in the Cleveland Clinic Colorectal Surgery department. He became Associate Professor in 2011 and Professor in 2017. Since January 2020, he has been working as a General Surgery Faculty Member at Medipol University Pendik Hospital.

Prof. Cem Terzi M.D.
Qualification Board Supervisor

Cem Terzi was born in 1962 in Ankara. He graduated from Ankara University Faculty of Medicine in 1984. He became a general surgery specialist in Ankara Numune Training and Research Hospital in 1992. He worked as a researcher and surgery specialist at Southamptan Faculty of Medicine Hospital in England for 2 years. Cem Terzi received the document (General Medical Council full registration in the United Kingdom) in 1997, that granted him the right to work as a doctor in England.

He became assistant professor in 1999, associate professor in 2000 and professor in 2005 at Dokuz Eylül University Faculty of Medicine. He retired in 2018 and started working as a freelance physician. He still works in his office in Izmir.

Prof. Ersin Öztürk M.D.
Virtual Academy / Social Media Supervisor

Ersin Öztürk, who was born in Bursa in 1974, graduated from Istanbul University Cerrahpaşa Faculty of Medicine in 1992 after completing his primary, secondary and high school education in Bursa. He started with his first choice in TUS (Exam for Specialty in Medicine), Uludağ University Faculty of Medicine, Department of General Surgery in 2000, he became a specialist in 2005, Assistant Professor in 2009 and Associate Professor in 2011 at the same clinic. He completed his doctorate at Uludağ University Faculty of Medicine, Department of Physiology between 2005-2009.

He worked as a research fellow in The Cleveland Clinic, Ohio for one year in 2008. In 2012, he received a sub-specialty in Surgical Oncology. In 2014, he successfully completed the exams in Barcelona and received the European Board of Surgery Qualification in Coloproctology (EBSQ-c) certificate. In 2019, he was appointed as a Professor at KTO Karatay University Faculty of Medicine, Department of General Surgery.

Ersin Öztürk has 56 articles published in journals recognized by international indexes and 16 articles published in national peer-reviewed journals. He has 35 papers presented at international congresses and 55 presentations at national congresses. One of these works received awards in international and seven in national congresses. He received the Turkish Surgical Competence Certificate in 2005 and was recertified in 2015.

Prof. Fahrettin Acar M.D.
Journal Supervisor / Public Relations

After graduating from Konya Selcuk University Faculty of Medicine in 1994, Prof. Dr. Fahrettin Acar completed his General Surgery Specialization training in Selcuk University Meram Medical Faculty General Surgery Service between 1994-2004. He was appointed to Selçuk University Faculty of Medicine as Assistant Professor in 2008 and received the title of Associate Professor in 2015.

In 2019, he received training on Minimally Invasive Surgery at the Cleveland Clinic Colorectal Surgery Department. He received the title of Professor in the field of General Surgery in 2020 and still continues to work as a General Surgery Faculty Member at Selçuk University Faculty of Medicine.

Prof. Sezai Leventoğlu M.D.
International Relations

Sezai Leventoglu, MD, is consultant of endoscopy, proctology and stomatherapy units at Gazi University School of Medicine. He is a Professor of Surgery at Gazi University School of Medicine.

Dr. Leventoglu specializes in treating patients with benign anorectal disease (especially fistula ani), pelvic floor disorders, colorectal cancer, Crohn's disease, and ulcerative colitis. He performs minimally invasive procedures such as robotic and laparoscopic colorectal resections, pelvic floor recostructions, transanal endoscopic microsurgery (TEM) and transanal minimally invasive surgery (TAMIS). He has a special interest in complex perianal fistulas such as horseshoe fistula and rectovaginal fistula. Dr. Leventoglu is also a national expert in the surgical management of benign anorectal disease such as complex anal fistula (horseshoe fistula), anal fissure, hemorrhoidal disease, and pelvic floor disorders such as fecal incontinence, rectal prolapse and constipation.

Dr. Leventoglu has published extensively in academic research journals on colorectal surgery. He is the co-editor of the book Anorectal Region Benign Diseases (on behalf of The Turkish Society of Colon and Rectal Surgery) published in 2011. He lectures both locally and internationally on invited topics including benign anorectal disease, pelvic floor disorders, ulcerative and Crohn's colitis, and rectal cancer.

In addition, he is a member of numerous surgical societies including the Turkish Surgery Association (TSA), a The Turkish Society of Colon and Rectum Surgery (TKRCD), the European Society of Coloproctology (ESCP), the American Society of Colon and Rectal Surgeons (ASCRS), the American College of Surgeons (ACS) and International Society University Colon and Rectal Surgeons (ISUCRS).

Prof. Feza Yarbuğ Karakayalı M.D.
Member / Congress President

Feza Karakayalı, born in 1973 in Niğde, graduated from Çukurova University Faculty of Medicine in 1996. She completed her General Surgery education in Ankara University Faculty of Medicine, Department of General Surgery between 1997-2003. In 2010, she received training in Minimally Invasive Colorectal Surgery and Pelvic Floor Diseases at the Cleveland Clinic Colorectal Surgery Department.

Between 2004-2014, she worked as a lecturer in Ankara Başkent University Faculty of Medicine, Department of General Surgery. She received the title of Associate Professor in the field of General Surgery in 2010 and the title of Professor in the field of General Surgery in 2015. She has been working as a General Surgery Faculty Member at Başkent University Istanbul Hospital since September 2014.
